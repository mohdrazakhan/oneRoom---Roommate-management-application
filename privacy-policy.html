Privacy Policy - OneRoom

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

Welcome to OneRoom. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our roommate management and communication application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, phone number, and profile photo
  • Room details such as room name, room members, and room code
  • Chat messages, images, media shared between room members
  • Expense records, settlements, and financial details
  • Task assignments, reminders, and completion status

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (OS version, device model, unique IDs)
  • App usage patterns & analytics
  • Firebase Instance ID (for push notifications)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

C. Third-Party Services We Use

  • Firebase Authentication (account creation & login)
  • Firebase Firestore (storage of user and room data)
  • Firebase Storage (media uploads - photos, files)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (push notifications)
  • Google AdMob (displaying ads)
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases such as Remove Ads)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your OneRoom account
  • To enable chat between room members
  • To manage expenses, settlements, and tasks
  • To send reminders, updates, and push notifications
  • To improve app performance and fix issues
  • To show advertisements via AdMob
  • To ensure app security and prevent fraud

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share information:

  • With other members of your room (for app functionality)
  • With Google Firebase services
  • With AdMob for delivering ads
  • When required by law
  • With your explicit consent

4. Data Security

Your data is securely stored using Firebase. We apply encryption, security rules, and industry-standard protection to safeguard personal data against unauthorized access.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you request account deletion, your data will be permanently erased from our servers within 30 days.

How to request account deletion:

Email us at: care.oneroom@gmail.com

6. In-App Purchases & Ads

OneRoom displays ads using Google AdMob unless you purchase the Remove Ads upgrade. Payment information is processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not store any card or banking details.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page, and the updated date will be reflected at the top.
