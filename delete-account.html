Delete Your OneRoom Account

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

If you want to delete your OneRoom account and all associated data, you can request deletion using the steps provided below. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information (name, email, phone number, profile photo)
  • Room membership and participation
  • Chat messages, shared media, and polls
  • Expenses, settlements, and financial records
  • Tasks, reminders, and completion history
  • Device + usage data linked to your Firebase profile

Data That May Be Temporarily Retained

Some data (such as backups or logs) may be stored for up to 30 days for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance. After this period, it will be permanently deleted.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can delete your account using any one of these methods:

Option 1 — In-App Deletion (Recommended)

Open the app → Go to Profile → Account → Delete Account. After confirmation, your account will be deleted within 24–48 hours.

Option 2 — Request Deletion by Email

Send an email to:

Email: care.oneroom@gmail.com

Your email must include:

  • Your registered email or phone number
  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 24–48 hours. All associated data will be deleted within up to 7 days.
